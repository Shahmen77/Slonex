Payment Security Policy

1. General Provisions

1.1. This Payment Security Policy (hereinafter referred to as the "Policy") defines the main principles and measures for protecting user data (hereinafter referred to as the "User") when making payments using the Company's online service, provided by PT Dewata Global Group (hereinafter referred to as the "Company").

1.2. By using the service to make payments, the User confirms their agreement with the terms of this Policy. If the User does not agree with the terms, they must refrain from using the payment functions of the service.

2. Protection of Personal Data During Payment

2.1. When processing payments, the Company ensures the security of Users' personal data in accordance with the laws of Indonesia and international standards.

2.2. All data provided by the User during payment is transmitted via secure channels using modern encryption tools.

2.3. Access to payment data is granted only to specially authorized employees and/or partners of the Company, and only to the extent necessary to process the payment or refund.

3. Use of Payment Cards and Electronic Means

3.1. Only those payment cards and electronic means that comply with the requirements of international payment systems and the law are accepted for payment for services on the Company's website.

3.2. When making a payment, the User is redirected to a secure payment page of the payment partner, where the payment card or other payment details are entered directly.

3.3. The Company does not store or process full bank card details of Users on its servers.

4. Refunds

4.1. Refund issues are governed in accordance with the user agreement and refund policy posted on the Company's website.

4.2. To request a refund, the User must send a request to the support service, providing the information necessary to identify the payment and process the refund.
